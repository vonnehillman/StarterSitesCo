About DigitalSacristan

DigitalSacristan is here to serve the behind the scenes, much like the role of a Sacristan, so that the work of the parish can be accomplished. DigitalSacristan is here to bring an awareness of parishes and ministries share their mission through vibrant, user-friendly websites. We take the stress out of maintaining your digital presence so you can focus on the sacrements, the work amongst the people, and prayer.

What Makes Us Different?

  • First hand knowledge of the business of parish life and the need to have help from the community and for the community!
  • Simple, clean layouts people actually want to visit
  • We handle updates, hosting, and help when you need it
